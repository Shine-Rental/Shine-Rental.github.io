Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place a rental order by submitting the requesting information in the Contact Us form, including the SKU number of the product(s) you would like to rent.

We will then reach out to you via the phone number you provided to coordinate a pickup time for your imitation jewellery order.

When you pick up the item(s), you will be asked for your Aadhar card and a refundable security deposit (see more below under "What do I need to bring with me when picking up my order?").

 

What do I need to bring with me when picking up my order?

You must the following items with you when you pick up your items.

  1. 1. Aadhar card, which will be scanned and associated with you order.
  2. 2. Refundable security deposit. The amount of the security deposit can be found associated with the product you select to rent. It is refundable after return of the item(s) in the same quality as they were rented.
  3. 3. Please also come prepared to sign a rental agreement, which includes a damage waiver.
  4. 4. If your rental amount is higher than 5,000 INR , you will be required to bring a cancelled check.

 

How are the items priced?

The listed rental price is per day (48 hour period). You will be charged for the next day if you are late returning your item(s), so please contact us in advance if you need to make adjustments to your return time/date. We also require a refundable security deposit which is included in the price. The GST taxes are not included in the item and will be added to the total.

 

Why do I need to sign a rental agreement?

Because we are a small family-run business, any damage or lost products means we have fewer items to rent in the future. We ask all of our customers for a fully refundable security deposit and a signed rental agreement so that we can maintain our stock and to ensure our customers fully understand our rental policies.

 

Are discounts available?

Yes, discounts are available on some items when purchased as a set or group. Contact Us for details.

 

What methods of payment do you accept?

We accept payment through phone pay, google pay and cash.
